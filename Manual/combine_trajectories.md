<h1>combine_trajectories</h1>

Creates a new list of trajectories by merging a set of existing trajectory_lists (the original trajectory_lists are not removed and remain accessible).

_combine_trajectories \<name of new trajectory\_list\> \<name of 1st existing trajectory\_list to merge\> \<name of 2nd existing trajectory_list to merge\> ... \<name of last existing trajectory\_list to merge\>_
